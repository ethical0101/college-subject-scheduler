# College Subject Scheduler

A premium, interactive web application to browse college courses, view slot structures, and construct clash-free timetables.

## Features
- **Curriculum Filtering**: View courses grouped by University Core, Program Core, and Program Electives.
- **Dynamic Search**: Filter by course code, title, faculty member, or slot name in real-time.
- **Interactive Timetable**: Click to add/remove classes, hover to highlight matching slots on the weekly calendar.
- **Real-Time Clash Detection**: Automatically detects conflicts between theory classes, lab classes, or theory-lab overlaps, keeping your schedule fully conflict-free.
- **Credit Calculator**: Automatically sums your total registered credits.
- **LocalStorage Persistence**: Saves your timetable state locally so your schedule is preserved across browser refreshes.
- **Image Export & Print Friendly**: Export your timetable as an image or print/save it as a PDF with a clean layout.
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Premium, modern interface with theme toggles.

## How to Run
1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2. Select your desired courses from the sidebar.
3. Choose your preferred faculty and slot combination to add it to your visual timetable.
4. Export or print your finalized schedule!
